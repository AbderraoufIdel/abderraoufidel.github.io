About Me

I’m Abderraouf Idel, a passionate medical doctor turned software developer and data analyst. My journey began with eight years in medical school, with three years spent studying in Constantine and five years in Batna, Algeria. During my medical education, I developed a deep interest in technology, starting with web development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript), which soon expanded into app development with C# and Xamarin.

However, it wasn’t until I discovered Python that I found my true passion. With Python, I immersed myself in machine learning through Andrew Ng’s course, which opened doors to the world of data analytics. Along the way, I explored various domains, including cryptocurrency and trading before the Bitcoin boom, though I later refocused on completing my medical studies. Even during that intense period, I continued to learn other skills, such as digital marketing and e-commerce.

Professional Experience

While I haven’t had extensive industry experience due to my medical studies, I’ve worked on a variety of smaller projects. These include projects in healthcare like MedPlus, several e-commerce stores, and numerous websites—some built from scratch, others through platforms like WordPress. My hands-on experience across these fields has helped me hone a unique combination of skills spanning healthcare, software development, and digital marketing.

Career Goals

As for the future? The sky is the limit. I aspire to combine my expertise in healthcare and technology to build something revolutionary, potentially a tech/healthcare company that could transform industries in Algeria and beyond.
